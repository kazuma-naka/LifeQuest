Color Challenge Game

Test your focus and color recognition skills! Read the word that appears, not the color it's displayed in. This game is based on the famous Stroop Effect, which measures your cognitive flexibility and attention control.

How to play: Look at the word displayed on the card and select the color that the TEXT says, not the color it appears in. You have 20 rounds to complete. The difficulty increases as you progress!
