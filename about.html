Why we built LifeQuest

About LifeQuest

Mission

LifeQuest helps users develop practical life skills through modular tracking and visible progress indicators.

Modules

  • Money Manager: budgeting and savings goals
  • Healthy Habits: wellness logs and consistency
  • Productivity Arena: goals and focus sessions
  • Achievements: badges and rewards

How “Leveling Up” Works (Concept)

Points
Earned by completing daily actions such as logging an entry or finishing a focus session.
Levels
Each skill area has a level that increases as points accumulate.
Badges
Unlocked by streaks, milestones, or consistent tracking.

Accessibility & Privacy

LifeQuest aims for clear structure, readable content, and accessible forms. Data handling details will be finalized in later phases.
